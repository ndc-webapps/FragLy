What is FragLy?

FragLy is a free Valorant stats tracker and performance coach built for competitive players. Enter any Riot ID and instantly pull real match data straight from the official Valorant API — no login, no setup, no cost. FragLy turns raw match history into clear, actionable insight: your true combat score, headshot percentage, win rates by agent and map, recent form trends, and a personalized AI breakdown of what's holding your rank back. Whether you're grinding from Iron to Radiant or just want to understand why you lost that last close game, FragLy gives you the numbers and the plan to improve.

How FragLy Works

1

Enter your Riot ID

Type your name and tag (for example PlayerName#TAG) and pick your region. That's the only thing FragLy ever asks for.

2

We pull your real matches

FragLy fetches your recent competitive games from the official Valorant data API and calculates every stat from the actual rounds you played — never estimated or faked.

3

Read your report & train

Study your performance radar, map and agent breakdowns, and AI coaching notes — then jump into the built-in Aim Trainer to sharpen the exact skills your stats say you're missing.
