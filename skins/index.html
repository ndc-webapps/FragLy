About the FragLy Skins Gallery

This gallery mirrors Riot's real weapon skin catalog — every skin's tier (Select, Deluxe, Premium, Exclusive, Ultra), its real render art, and — for the higher tiers that have one — Riot's own animated preview video, played directly from their CDN. Nothing here is fan art or a mockup.

True 3D rotation isn't something Riot exposes publicly, so where a skin has a real animated preview, it plays inline. Otherwise you get the real high-resolution render and can switch between every official color variant. Want to check your own loadout stats instead? Search your Riot ID on FragLy.
